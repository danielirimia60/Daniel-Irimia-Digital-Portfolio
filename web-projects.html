Stephenson's Historic House - A tourism themed website prototype inspired by the life of George Stephenson.

  • PROJECT: Stephenson's Historic House
  • DEMO: github.io/SHH-Website

Stephenson's Historic House is a touristic website meant to showcase and promote the places where George Stephenson lived. It is a static website with ample amounts of information for new and older tourists. The website is a prototype not an official webpage, being just a college project.

Fotomat - A website promoting their camera brand, aimed at photography enthusiasts all over the world.

  • PROJECT: Fotomat
  • DEMO: github.io/Fotomat

Fotomat is a project meant to promote a new brand of camera devices, lenses and filters that is aimed at classical photography enthusiasts. This project was created as part of the front-end development course at Codeacademy.

Tea Cozy - This website promotes a new chain of tea and coffe shops in Portland, aimed at those interested in healthy products.

  • PROJECT: Tea Coy
  • DEMO: github.io/Tea-Cozy

Tea Cozy is a website that promotes a new franchise in Portland, Oregon which specializes in healthy beverages like tea and coffee and other seasonal products. This project was created as part of the front-end development course at Codeacademy.
